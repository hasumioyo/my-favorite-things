French Fries

French fries are deep-fried strips of potato that are typically salted and served hot. They are a favorite fast food item and side dish, commonly enjoyed with ketchup, mayonnaise, or various other sauces. To make them, potatoes are sliced into thin strips, fried until they turn golden brown, and then seasoned. Popular around the globe, French fries come in various styles, making them a beloved snack for many people.
